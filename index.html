Delete Your CallMe Account

We respect your right to privacy and provide an easy way to delete your CallMe account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request account deletion, please send an email to: farhanmeo2@gmail.com with the subject line: Delete My CallMe Account.

Include the following information in your email:

  • Your registered email address or username.
  • Reason for deletion (optional).

What Happens Next

Once we verify your request, we will begin the deletion process. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 14 business days.

Data Retention Policy

We do not retain your personal data after deletion unless required by law or for fraud prevention purposes. In such cases, minimal necessary data may be securely stored as per legal obligations.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about account deletion or your data, feel free to reach out to our support team at the email above.
