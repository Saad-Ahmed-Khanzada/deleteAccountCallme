Delete Your CallMe Account

We value your privacy and provide a straightforward way to delete your CallMe account and associated data.

In-App Account Deletion

You can delete your account directly within the CallMe app:

  1. Open the CallMe app on your device.
  2. Navigate to Profile Tab.
  3. Select Delete Account and follow the on-screen instructions.

Data Deletion Policy

Upon initiating account deletion:

  • Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 14 business days.
  • We do not retain your personal data after deletion unless required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention).

Need Assistance?

If you encounter any issues or have questions about the deletion process, please contact our support team at info@callme.au.
